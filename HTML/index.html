Our story

Built by athletes,
for athletes.

ApexFIT AI began when three sports scientists and two Olympic coaches asked a simple question: why do elite athletes have access to tools that everyday athletes never get?

The mission that drives everything

We believe every person who trains deserves the same quality of coaching intelligence available to the world's best athletes. Not approximations. Not generic templates. Real, adaptive, scientifically validated guidance — personalized to your specific biology.

Founded in 2019 by Ababalwe Jali, Jonathan Millz, and Mellisa Hudson, ApexFIT AI has grown from a research prototype into the platform trusted by 2.4 million athletes across 140 countries.

Our team combines deep sports science expertise with world-class machine learning engineering — united by a single obsession: making you better, every single day.
